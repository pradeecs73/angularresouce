What is the role of a mentor?

A mentor may help with exploring careers, setting goals, developing contacts, and identifying resources. The knowledge, advice, and resources a mentor shares depend on the format and goals of a specific mentoring relationship. A mentor may share with a mentee (or protege) information about his or her own career path, as well as provide guidance, motivation, emotional support, and role modeling.

Benefits for Mentors

1. Recognition as a subject matter expert and leader.
2. Exposure to fresh perspectives, ideas and approaches.
3. Extension of their professional development record.
4. Opportunity to reflect on their own goals and practices.
5. Development of their personal leadership and coaching styles.
